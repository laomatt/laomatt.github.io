Battle-Hangman

In this game you must guess you're opponents word letter by letter while your opponent does the same with a word that you pick, all the while wagering points on hit and misses. When one word is guessed, the game ends and the person who guessed the game gets a bonus in points, but doesn't nessesarily win, victory is decided by who has the most points at end-game.

javascript, html, css

Lets play!

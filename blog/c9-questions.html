Questions

Febuary 20th, 2015

I regularly ask questions on stack-overflow. Its a good way to get a specific answer.

Sometimes people mark it as a duplicate, and I don't get as much feed back.

I find that the best way to get more answers is to be as clear as possible. And take time to format your code clearly.

Also you must be very compact in your code and strip it down to what the question needs to illustrate its point, as large amounts of reading tend to turn off many forum readers.
